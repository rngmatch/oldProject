The Third Visit to Japan

The third trip had a surprising twist when my friend was unable to join me for this trip, so I experienced my first solo overseas trip and attend a concert held at the Nippon Budokan. Originally, I had planned to visit Kyoto, but due to a sudden change put in place by the Japanese law, the apartment I rented was unavailable. Additionally, I experienced an earthquake for the first time during this visit, from the apartment in Tokyo, I only felt a small shaking.
